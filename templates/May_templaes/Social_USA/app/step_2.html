The Fragrance Story

The House of Creed is proud to present
Viking Cologne.

A juxtaposition to its former fiery
fragrance, this cooler counterpart is a
pioneering universal scent that longs for
adventure, travel and the unknown.

An ode to the natural world, Viking Cologne
is an aromatic fougère; a breathtaking fresh
fragrance with a real marine appearance.

This masterful alchemy of woody
sandalwood, fresh bergamot, nutmeg and
frankincense combines the headiness of Eau
de Parfum with the invigorating freshness of
cologne. A universal fragrance of boundless
exploration.

We invite you to discover Viking
Cologne with a complimentary 2 ml
sample, courtesy of The House of Creed
